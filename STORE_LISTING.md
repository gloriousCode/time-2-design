# TrioWay App Store Listing Draft

## Short Description

Three-colour geometric Pebble Time 2 watchface with analogue and digital layouts, configurable complications, and curated muted presets.

## Full Description

TrioWay is a watchface for Pebble Time 2 that blends clean geometry with practical time layouts.

Features:

- Analogue, Digital Small, and Digital Large modes
- Three dynamic background sectors driven by hour and minute position
- Optional 3 o'clock rounded-square date complication
- Optional 6 o'clock day/date tiles
- Optional analogue hands (including in analogue mode toggle)
- Preset themes tuned for Pebble Time 2 colour constraints
- Full colour customisation for sectors, hands, and complications

Default style: `sandGraphite` with 3 o'clock complication enabled.

## What's New in 1.1.0
